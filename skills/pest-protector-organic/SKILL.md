# Pest Protector Organic

Organic pest protection strategies for sustainable pest management.

## Purpose

Pest pressure destroys crops: aphids transmit diseases, caterpillars defoliate plants, beetles damage roots, mites cause stunted growth. Chemical pesticides are expensive and harmful. Organic protection requires knowledge and timing. This skill provides organic pest protection strategies.
