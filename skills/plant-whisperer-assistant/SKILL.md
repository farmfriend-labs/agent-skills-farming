# Plant Whisperer Assistant

Plant health monitoring and advice for garden and small farm operations.

## Purpose

Plant health is complex: nutrient deficiencies manifest as subtle symptoms, pest damage confused with disease, water stress mistaken for nutrient issues. By the time symptoms are obvious, it's often too late. This skill provides early plant health monitoring and advice.
