# Repair Decision Assistant

Help farmers make informed repair vs replace decisions for equipment.

## Purpose

Equipment repair decisions are complex: repair cost vs equipment value, age of equipment, expected remaining life, repair frequency, downtime costs, replacement costs. Wrong decisions cost thousands. This skill provides comprehensive analysis to make informed repair decisions.
