# One Screen Mission Control

Single-screen operations control center for farm management.

## Purpose

Farm operations scattered across multiple systems: GPS guidance on one screen, yield monitor on another, weather on a tablet, equipment monitoring on a phone, records in a notebook. Critical information is inaccessible when needed. This skill provides unified mission control.
