# Plug and Play Precision Agriculture

Simple, affordable precision agriculture setup for smaller operations.

## Purpose

Precision agriculture is too complex and expensive for smaller farms: expensive hardware, complex setup, technical expertise required, ongoing subscriptions. Small farmers are left behind while technology benefits large operations. This skill provides simple, plug-and-play precision agriculture tools that work out of the box without technical expertise.
