# Field History Intelligence

Track and analyze field history data for informed decisions.

## Purpose

Field knowledge is cumulative: yield history, input history, pest history, soil test results, weather patterns, equipment operations. Forgotten history leads to repeated mistakes. This skill maintains comprehensive field history and provides intelligence for decisions.
